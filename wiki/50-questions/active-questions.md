# Active Questions

These are the current questions that should guide further reading and ingestion.

1. How much of recent reasoning performance comes from RL versus pretraining versus inference-time compute?
2. Which post-training recipes are robust across open models, and which are highly model-specific?
3. What are the practical limits of long-context usage compared with retrieval-heavy systems?
4. Which agent benchmarks are closest to real work, and where do they still distort reality?
5. How should reward models evolve when judge models themselves become reasoning systems?
